About me

For me music is not the most important thing in my life but I enjoy listening to musics. It’s a nice thing to have but if I don’t have it, or if it’s not available, I won’t fret about it. I also don’t have particular favorite genre, I listen to all of them. Good lyrics are always captivating my attention, so i think lyrics are what’s most important for me when it comes to music. And when i say “good”, doesn’t mean it’s poetic, it’s just I find them meaningful or inspiring or related to my life.
